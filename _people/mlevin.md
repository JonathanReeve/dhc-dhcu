Meredith Levin has assumed the role of Managing Editor of Digital Dante as of 2016. She is the Interim Head of the Burke Library at the Union Theological Seminary and the Western European Humanities Librarian at Columbia University. She holds a BA in English Literature from New York University, an MS in Library and Information Science from Long Island University, and an MA in Italian Studies from New York University. She has presented at the American Association of Italian Studies, the Italian American Studies Association, and the Modern Language Association and her work on emerging technologies has been published by the American Library Association.
